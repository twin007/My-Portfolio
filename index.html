About Me

Hi, I’m Twinkle Kaushik — a data enthusiast who believes that behind every chart lies a story waiting to be told.

What drew me to the world of data? Honestly, it’s the thrill of the end result — watching messy datasets transform into clear, meaningful insights that empower better decisions. Whether I’m building dashboards, training models, or scrubbing raw data into shape, I love the moment when it all clicks — and I can help others truly “see” the data.

I’m especially passionate about using data for good, with a keen interest in healthcare and education. From exploring patterns in public health to designing dashboards that help teachers and students thrive — I aim to bring clarity where it matters most.

I live by the motto: “Everything happens for a reason.” And in data? That reason is often measurable.

Currently open to full-time opportunities where I can solve problems, visualize outcomes, and keep learning every day. Let’s turn numbers into narratives together.
